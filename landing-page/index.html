Questions

Common questions

Short answers before you start.

Who is Simple Roster Plus for?

Managers at shift-based small businesses who need to publish a weekly schedule and track attendance without a bloated HR system. If you want payroll, hiring, and benefits in one suite, this isn't that—and that's intentional.

How does Auto Scheduler help?

Auto Scheduler speeds up the repetitive parts—suggesting shift fills, surfacing exceptions, and keeping the view focused on what to do next—so you spend less time clicking and more time running the floor.

How long does setup take?

We keep onboarding light: add your team, set how clock-ins work, and build your first week. Most managers are scheduling within minutes, not days.

Can we connect clock-in hardware?

Yes—many teams use terminals (including ZKTeco) alongside the app. The goal is one clear manager view of plan vs. actual, not another disconnected spreadsheet.

Will this replace our HR or payroll software?

No. Simple Roster Plus is for weekly scheduling and attendance. You can export hours for whoever runs payroll; we don't process pay or file taxes.

How much does it cost?

Free covers up to 10 staff and a 30-day device sync trial. Plus is $19.99/month (or $199/year) for up to 50 staff, live attendance, and SMS roster publish. Pro is $49.99/month for up to 100 staff with more devices and admins. See pricing for details.

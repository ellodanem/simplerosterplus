Questions

Common questions

Short answers before you start your trial.

Who is Simple Roster Plus for?

Managers at shift-based small businesses who need to publish a weekly schedule and track attendance without a bloated HR system. If you want payroll, hiring, and benefits in one suite, this isn’t that—and that’s intentional.

How does AI help?

AI speeds up the repetitive parts—suggesting shift fills, surfacing exceptions, and keeping the UI focused on what to do next—so you spend less time clicking and more time running the floor.

How long does setup take?

We keep onboarding light: add your team, set how clock-ins work, and you’re ready to build the first week. Most managers should be scheduling within minutes, not days of configuration.

Will this replace our HR or payroll software?

No. Simple Roster Plus is for weekly scheduling and attendance. You can export hours for whoever runs payroll; we don’t process pay or file taxes.

Can we connect clock-in hardware?

Yes—many teams use terminals (including ZKTeco) alongside the app. The goal is one clear manager view of plan vs. actual, not another disconnected spreadsheet.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is still being finalized. Start a free trial to use the product; we’ll publish simple plans before paid launch. No payroll or enterprise HR bundles—just roster and attendance for managers.

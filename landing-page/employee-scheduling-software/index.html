Employee scheduling questions

What Managers Ask Before They Start

Can I copy last week's employee schedule?

Yes. Copy the previous week's eligible shifts into the new week, then adjust what changed. Approved leave, closed holidays, and locked days are respected.

Can I create reusable shifts?

Yes. Save shift names, start and end times, unpaid breaks, and colors so familiar hours are quick to assign again.

Can I see leave while building the roster?

Yes. Approved vacation and approved days off remain visible and block those roster cells. Closed holidays can block assignments too.

Can employees see their schedules?

Staff can open the published read-only full-roster link. Simple Roster Plus does not currently provide a personalized authenticated employee schedule portal.

Does it support multiple locations?

Roster weeks and staff can be tied to a location. The Free plan supports up to two locations; Plus and Pro support more. The product does not present every location in one combined scheduling view.

Does Simple Roster Plus track attendance too?

Yes. Scheduled shifts provide expected times for attendance, helping managers see scheduled, present, late, and absent status. Attendance supports the roster—it is not payroll automation.

Is Auto Scheduler available?

Auto Scheduler is coming soon. Today, managers can copy the previous week and adjust the roster manually.
